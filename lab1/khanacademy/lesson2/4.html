Travel to Hawaii

Hawaii is an island state in the Western United States, about 2,000 miles (3,200 km) from the U.S. mainland in the Pacific Ocean. It is the only U.S. state outside North America, the only one which is an archipelago, and the only one in the tropics.

Etymology

The State of Hawaii derives its name from the name of its largest island, Hawaiʻi.
A common explanation of the name of Hawaiʻi is that it was named for Hawaiʻiloa, a figure from Hawaiian oral tradition.
He is said to have discovered the islands when they were first settled.
